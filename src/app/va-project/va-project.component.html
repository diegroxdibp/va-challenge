TRIENAL DE AQUITECTURA DE LISBOA

Open House is an architecture event that takes place in many cities across the world. During a weekend, selected public and private spaces open their doors for thousands of visitors. In this fast-paced event, everyone wants to visit as many locations as possible, but the available places are limited.

In past editions, this led to confusion, long waiting lines and an uneven frequency between different venues. So we partnered with Open House Lisbon's organisation to create a web platform with a mobile-first experience that works as a field guide to plan a schedule, find the closest, less crowded venues and quickly get there.
